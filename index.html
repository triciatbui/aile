Rise is on a mission to empower tactile learners and promote universally accessible design. With over 285 million people worldwide who are visually impaired, less than 10% of that demographic knows braille. Our goal is to rise braille literacy with an accessible, affordable and portable platform to learning braille, and provide valued social currency among this population.
