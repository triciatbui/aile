V.001.01 NOTE

To me, there’s not much that’s more beautiful than a connection. Whether it’s in the form of a relationship, like a mother and son, being in tune with yourself, or having an affinity to nature, these experiences create a sense of harmony and peace. I’ve found the power of connection to be even more important recently, with the blessing of my baby niece, Adeline, who’s turning two this month! I have the honor of being her Godmother and am so excited to find ways to nurture her development and creativity. And yes! It takes a lot of effort to fully attend to another human being (hah!), but I’ve found that when I am really present with Adeline, or anyone for that matter, in the end, I am the one that’s rewarded with more energy and fulfillment. I cannot stress how important a strong bond between a parent and child is to allow that child’s development to thrive. The value of putting your love into action is beyond measure.

In this issue, we explore themes of connection - Connecting with your child(ren), connecting with yourself, connecting mind and body. This issue has carefully curated materials for each developmental skill, chosen with intention. For cognitive development, this issue focuses on processing and applying memory and how the brain connects that information to perform an activity. For speech and language development, we explore how language is connected to the expression of wants and needs. For sensory development, we dig into the connection between a physical object and sensational feeling. For motor development, we look into the ability of connecting both sides of the body to perform tasks. Our mission is to deliver quality material to promote your little one’s cognitive, speech language, sensory and motor development, and ultimately your relationship with your little peanut(s). Cheers! - Tricia Bui, Editor
